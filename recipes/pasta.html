Pasta Pasta Pisa!

Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian pasta dish celebrated for its simplicity and robust flavors.
recipe involves sautéing minced or sliced garlic in olive oil until golden and tossing the aromatic mixture with cooked pasta. The result is a
delightful dish that beautifully showcases the essence of Italian cuisine through the marriage of garlic and high-quality olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or your preferred pasta
  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley
  • Parmesan or pecorino cheese
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta cooking water before draining.

  2. In a large skillet, heat a generous amount of extra-virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced or sliced garlic to the oil and sauté until it becomes fragrant and golden. Be cautious not to burn the garlic.

  3. If you desire a bit of heat, add red pepper flakes to the garlic and oil mixture. Adjust the quantity based on your spice preference.

  4. Once the pasta is cooked, transfer it directly from the pot to the skillet with the garlic and oil. Toss the pasta to coat it evenly with the flavorful mixture.

  5. If the pasta seems dry, add a bit of the reserved pasta cooking water gradually to achieve the desired consistency.

  6. Season the dish with salt and black pepper to taste. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the pasta for a burst of color and freshness.

  7. Plate the Aglio e Olio pasta and, if desired, top it with grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese.
