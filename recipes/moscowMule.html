Moscow Mule


Donkeys, Mules, and Moscow

A Moscow Mule is a refreshing and iconic cocktail known for its distinctive copper mug presentation. This classic drink typically consists of three main ingredients: vodka, ginger beer, and lime juice. The
cocktail is served over ice and garnished with a wedge of lime. The combination of the crisp, spicy kick from the ginger beer, the zesty acidity from the lime, and the smoothness of the vodka creates a
well-balanced and satisfying beverage. The Moscow Mule has become a popular choice for those seeking a flavorful and effervescent cocktail with a hint of nostalgia, often enjoyed in its signature copper mug.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Ginger beer
  • Lime juice
  • Lime wedge
  • Mint leaves

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients, including your preferred vodka, ginger beer, and fresh limes.
  2. Add ice cubes to a copper mug. The use of a copper mug is traditional and helps keep the drink cool.
  3. Pour 2 ounces of vodka over the ice in the copper mug.
  4. Squeeze the juice from approximately half a lime, yielding about 0.75 ounces. Adjust the amount based on your taste preference for acidity.
  5. Top the vodka and lime juice with 4-6 ounces of ginger beer, depending on the size of your mug and your desired level of ginger flavor.
  6. Use a stirring stick or spoon to gently mix the ingredients in the copper mug.
  7. If desired, garnish the Moscow Mule with a lime wedge on the rim of the mug. Some people also add a sprig of fresh mint for additional aroma.
  8. Your Moscow Mule is ready to be enjoyed! Sip and savor the refreshing combination of vodka, ginger beer, and lime.
