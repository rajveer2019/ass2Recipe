Margherita Pizza


Pisa Pizza Pisa!

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian pizza renowned for its simplicity and vibrant flavors. The pizza features a thin crust topped with a light tomato sauce made from fresh tomatoes, garlic, and basil. Generous slices
of fresh mozzarella cheese are layered on top, and the pizza is finished with whole basil leaves and a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil. This iconic combination creates a visually appealing pizza that pays homage to
the colors of the Italian flag â€“ red from the tomatoes, green from the basil, and white from the mozzarella. The Margherita pizza is a timeless favorite, celebrated for its deliciously straightforward and authentic taste.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Prepare dough
  3. Apply tomato sauce
  4. Apply mozzarella
  5. Scatter basil leaves
  6. Drizzle olive oil
  7. Bake in the oven
  8. Serve
